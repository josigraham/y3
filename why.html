The Mini Cooper Insol
Introducing the Mini Cooper Insol:
A solar-powered car that drives smoother than those that run on gasoline.
The Insol comes available in five colors, equipped with state-of-the-art interiors and solar panels.
The Insol’s innovative solar panels are able to retrieve as much energy from the sun as possible.

The creators of the Mini Cooper Insol understand that solar panels contain glass which adds weight to them.
That is why the unique design and photovoltaic cells within the our car convert the sun's energy directly into
electric energy, exceeding the added fuel consumption caused by the dense weight of the panels.

We aim for alternative-energy powered cars to become the industry standard. Insol owners will feel proud of their
contribution to the environment and the aesthetically pleasing look of their environmentally friendly car.
